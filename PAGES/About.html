Acerca de Ala Puerca

Perseverance and Passion

At Ala Puerca, we have overcome challenges and we are proud to serve you with passion and dedication. Our story is the story of perseverance and the love of good food.

Quality and Variety

We value quality in every dish we serve. Our menu is carefully selected to offer you a wide variety of delicious options that will satisfy all tastes and preferences.

Good Prices

We believe that good food does not have to be expensive. At Ala Puerca, we strive to offer you an excellent value for your money so that you can enjoy a satisfying culinary experience without breaking the bank.

After the Quarantine

After difficult times, we have emerged with renewed energy. We are delighted to welcome our customers back and offer them an unparalleled culinary experience after the quarantine.
